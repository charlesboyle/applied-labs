problem

Finding free, genuine events in the UAE means checking 20+ separate sites. Most event platforms bury free listings under paid ones, nothing covers all seven emirates, and community events from Reddit never make it onto official calendars.

what we built
01
Two-tier scraping

Standard scan pulls official venue calendars — Dubai Opera, Louvre Abu Dhabi, Sharjah Art Foundation. Deep scan scrapes Reddit communities (r/dubai, r/abudhabi, r/UAE) for grassroots events that never appear on official lists.

02
Location-first sorting

Browser Geolocation API pins your position. Every event is sorted by real distance. Free, this weekend, closest first. No account, no preferences wizard.

03
Community moderation

Built-in report flow surfaces spam, duplicates, and outdated listings to keep quality high as the event database grows.
